**Standard terms 2026**:
- 12-month cliff, 24-month linear vesting on tokens
- Token price at 1/10 to 1/30 of public launch price for angels
- $25k SAFT minimum for funds; $500-5k for angels

## 10. What NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't promise specific ROI** to investors ("you'll 100x") — securities risk
- ❌ **Don't sell tokens via Telegram bots / DMs without legal structure** — SEC will find you
- ❌ **Don't accept money from unknown wallets without KYC them** — AML risk
- ❌ **Don't use AI-generated outreach** that investors instantly recognize
- ❌ **Don't follow up >2x without new info** — once a "no" or silence x2, leave them alone
- ❌ **Don't take meetings with "investors" who DM YOU first asking for fees / setup costs** — 100% scammers
- ❌ **Don't sign a deal without a lawyer reviewing** — even $5k angel deals
- ❌ **Don't dilute beyond 25% in pre-seed** — leaves no room for later rounds
